Jobs at Cottage Labs

We’re a small company working on diverse projects including data repositories and infrastructure to support open access publishing. Much of our code is open source and we maintain links to the open data community worldwide. Our clients have included the Universities of Stanford, Oxford, and Edinburgh, as well as organisations such as Crossref, the Directory of Open Access Journals, and the World Nuclear Association. Projects run from a few months to years and usually involve development, customisation, and the deployment of data management and analysis platforms.

Software Engineer - Ruby on Rails

Cottage labs is looking for a UK based remote software engineer with at least two years of experience to join our team of data and software experts. Proximity to the rest of the team in Oxford, Milton Keynes or Edinburgh would be preferred for regular online calls and occasional in-person meetings.

The role

We’re looking for a self-motivated developer who’s worked as part of a software dev team before looking to contribute to a variety of projects for clients all over the world. Key responsibilities include:

  • Contributing high quality code, tests, and documentation to projects
  • Collaborating with project team members - discussing approaches to new features, reviewing pull requests, pair programming where applicable etc.
  • Communicating effectively with clients - attending meetings where possible, engaging with queries and technical questions relating to projects
  • Keeping up-to-date with best practice and promising new technologies

How we work

The whole team at Cottage Labs has always worked remotely. We regularly collaborate on projects by pairing over zoom and we have an active Slack workspace where support is available. So do people we work with; you may be hopping between channels talking to various interesting people throughout the day.

We meet up in person from time to time (expenses will be covered) and have a weekly catch-up during work hours to keep in touch. We’re also open to flexible working arrangements.

Our stack

As we have some ongoing work and prospects involving the Samvera Hyrax repository software, we’d like to increase our in-house expertise. In addition, Cottage Labs has a variety of long-term projects in numerous programming languages, so any additional languages and interests would benefit us. We also require knowledge of version control.

Demonstrated experience in digital library applications or related application domains would be a bonus.

Essential

  • Proficiency with Ruby on Rails
  • HTML & CSS
  • Some experience with JavaScript
  • Knowledge of version control

Desirable

  • Experience with Samvera, Hyrax
  • Solr or ElasticSearch
  • Git and GitHub
  • Additional languages such as Python, Node.js

In addition to development, it may be necessary to perform deployments and diagnosis of running systems, for which familiarity with Linux servers, remote access via SSH, and wider knowledge of web hosting would be useful.

Benefits

  • Remote working as standard
  • Flexible working hours
  • £35k - £55k depending on experience
  • Holiday allowance of 32 days including Bank Holidays

Recruitment Stages

  • Application
  • Informal conversation with partners about you, your interests, and the role
  • Technical interview & coding evaluation

To apply contact us at jobs@cottagelabs.com with your CV.
