Business Continuity Plan

Cottage Labs LLP has identified the provision of hosted software services to its clients as its critical task with respect to its corporate engagements. Cottage Labs is prepared for various scenarios which may cause a significant disruption of Cottage Labs' or its clients' business, and which may affect the hardware on which the services run. Types of disasters include but are not limited to:

  • Natural Causes - fires, hurricanes, earthquakes, floods, tornadoes
  • Technical causes - hardware/software failures
  • Human causes - riots, strikes

The contingency plan calls for Cottage Labs to relocate its mission critical systems to alternative operating facilities. In the event that mission critical systems have been lost or damaged, arrangements will be made to promptly replace such systems by drawing on the resources of Cottage Labs LLP.
