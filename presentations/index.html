Weave

Interactive publication and visualisation for all your data

Weave provides you with a way to publish your organisation's data so your users can interact with it in real-time, explore angles, and learn the answers to questions they didn't even know they had.

Whether you are a small company working with specialised datasets for select clientele, a research group with cutting-edge data to disseminate, or a large organisation with large datasets from multiple sources to present to the public or your clients, Weave can provide a way to make it understood.

We can help you with any stage of your project, including

  • Initial requirements capture and analysis
  • Data modelling and system architecture
  • Implementation and deployment of a Fedora/Hydra software stack
  • Hosting
  • Bespoke development, creating or customising existing components
  • 3rd party system integration - authentication, deposit environments, CRIS and CMS
  • Maintenance, upgrades and migrations

Our developers have many years experience in the repository, research data management and research information space, and can bring that to your project!
