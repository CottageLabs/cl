Open article resolver

(Like DOI and dx.doi.org, but no paywalls)

You want an article, but it is behind a paywall. Maybe it is available for free (legally) elsewhere! If it is, and we can find it, we will redirect you to the openly available version. If it is not, we will provide some options for requesting access to it.

Just use the DOI of any article that you want, but use cx.ctg.li/ instead of dx.doi.org/

https://cx.ctg.li/

Resolve it

This works with DOIs but also with PubMed or PubMedCentral IDs:
https://cx.ctg.li/10.1186/1758-2946-3-47
https://cx.ctg.li/PMID/21999661
https://cx.ctg.li/PMC/3206455

(dx.ctg.li will work too)

Help us stay up to date, let us know where to find things

We search many sources for free and legally available copies of research articles, but sometimes we can't find one. Did you know that even if you have given your research to be published in a closed access journal, it is quite often allowed to ALSO deposit it for open access? And also, even if that is not allowed, in many cases you can deposit a pre-print (the same article, just without the formatting for the specific journal) and people can access that for free. So if you have a URL for an article that we cannot find, please do let us know! Or if you have an article you would like to deposit, let us know that too, we will put a copy up online for you, or recommend a place where you can put it yourself if you prefer.
