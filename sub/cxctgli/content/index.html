Just use the DOI of any article that you want, but use cx.ctg.li/ instead of dx.doi.org/

cx.ctg.li/

Resolve it

Works like DOIs and dx.doi.org, but avoids paywalls.
Also works with PubMed or PubMedCentral IDs:
cx.ctg.li/10.1186/1758-2946-3-47
cx.ctg.li/PMID/21999661
cx.ctg.li/PMC/3206455

(dx.ctg.li will work too)

We search many sources for free and legally available copies of research articles, but sometimes we can't find one. Did you know that even if you have given your research to be published in a closed access journal, it is very often also possible to publish a version openly. So if you have an article that we cannot find, please do let us know! We'd be happy to help check what open publishing rights you have.
