Lantern gives you a quick way to gather all your Open Access compliance measures to help you meet institutional, funder and government mandates.

For each article you ask us about we can find information like the publisher's re-use licence, whether it is in a repoisitory (and which ones), its status in EuropePMC, embargo and self-archiving policies, as well as grant and P.I. information.

Lantern is a service provided by Cottage Labs, and is part of the Harvest collaboration.

To process one article, enter the DOI, PubMed ID, or PubMedCentral ID (prefixed with PMC) in the field above, or choose a file to upload. The format must be CSV, which can be exported by your spreadsheet software.
