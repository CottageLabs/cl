Lantern checks the licence conditions of published research. Search for any article identified by DOI, PMID, PMC, or title, or upload many in a spreadsheet; you'll receive an email once they're all done.

Lantern is a service provided by Cottage Labs, and is part of the Harvest collaboration.

To process one article, enter the DOI, PubMed ID, or PubMedCentral ID (prefixed with PMC) in the field above, or choose a file to upload. The format must be CSV, which can be exported by spreadsheet software.

Read the documentation for more details.
