URL shortenizer

(Like bit.ly but ctg.li)

You want a short URL? We get you short URL!

Just provide the URL of any article that you want, but use ctg.li/ instead of dx.doi.org/

For example:

https://ctg.li/ Shorten it, randomly!

Or try getting your own custom short address, if it is available:

https://ctg.li/ Shorten it, customly!

Once you press the button, we will check and confirm for you and return your short URL, then you can use that anywhere you like, and it will redirect to the full long URL.
