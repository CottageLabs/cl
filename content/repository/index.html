University of Hull

The University of Hull was carrying out some prototyping work to determine if they could create an easy research data deposit workflow, that would be unobtrusive and intuitive within the researcher's environment

We worked with the library to design a workflow which began with the researchers Box account, automatically picking up work to be archived, and passing it through the Archivematica preservation platform, and into the Hydra at Hull repository. We then constructed a number of new components to support this workflow, and deployed it to the Hull infrastructure, along with Archivematica, laying the groundwork for future research data management at the institution.
