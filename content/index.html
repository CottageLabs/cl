Clients

Our clients range from small organisations requiring specialist technical assistance, to large organisations exploring new developments for their business, and world-class universities working on cutting-edge information systems. We’d love to include you in the list.

What we do

Software Development - with experience in a broad range of software environments and languages, we can tackle any technical challenge you want to throw at us. From bespoke databases and applications, extensions to existing applications, to integrations between systems, we can help.

Consultancy - we are experienced information architects, gatherers and analysers of user requirements, and system designers. If you’re not sure what you need yet, we can help you find out.

Interactive data analysis and reporting - we can provide you with near out-of-the box data visualisation for your website or for delivery as reports to your clients or management.

Managed Hosting - we offer a full-stack managed application hosting environment, where we provide you everything from the machines running your software, app installation and health monitoring, fault diagnosis and backup/restore.

Cottaged Services

Lantern - Developed in collaboration with the Wellcome Trust, this service allows you to upload spreadsheets of research article identifiers and we’ll pull together all the Open Access compliance information we can about each one. If you’re looking to enhance your research organisation’s OA knowledge, this is the place to start.

The Open Article Gauge - Developed in collaboration with the Public Library of Science (PLOS), this service allows you to provide research article identifiers, and receive back information about the re-use licences associated with the work.
