Cottage Labs is a partnership of software development and data analysis experts who can help you build tailored solutions for your needs.

We offer a range of services to our clients, from software development and agile project management, to managed web services and sysadmin, and our own products and tools for you to take advantage of. Read on to learn more about us, and we’d love to hear from you to talk about collaboration.

Who we work with

Our clients range from small organisations requiring specialist technical assistance, to large organisations exploring new developments for their business, and world-class universities working on cutting-edge information systems. We’d love to include you in the list.

What we do

Software Development - with experience in a broad range of software environments and languages, we can tackle any technical challenge you want to throw at us. From bespoke databases and applications, and extensions to existing applications, to integrations between systems, we can help.

Consultancy - we are experienced information architects, analysers of user requirements, and system designers. If you’re not sure what you need yet, we can help you find out.

Interactive data analysis and reporting - we can provide you with real-time data visualisation for your website or for delivery as reports to your clients or management.

Managed Application Hosting - we offer a full-stack managed application hosting environment, where we provide you everything from the machines running your software, app installation and health monitoring, fault diagnosis and backup/restore.

Our products and tools

As part of our commitment to improving access to - and quality of - information on the web, we provide a number free, freemium and bespoke services for the community. Follow the links below, and hopefully you'll find something useful to you.

Lantern - Developed in collaboration with the Wellcome Trust, this service allows you to give us article identifiers and we’ll pull together all the Open Access compliance information we can about each one. If you’re looking to enhance your research organisation's OA knowledge, this is the place to start.

Willow - Building on our long term engagement with the repository community, we are pleased to offer Fedora/Hydra-based repository solutions to our clients.

Content Mine - Cottage Labs has partnered with expert academics and software developers to construct a combination of text mining software and targetted data mining training to help you extract the most from your research.

Weave - Weave provides you with a way to publish your organisation's data so your users can interact with it in real-time, explore angles, and learn the answers to questions they didn't even know they had.
