COTTAGE LABS 2016 CALENDAR

This year we're doing something a little different. We've gathered together great photos taken by Cottage Labs people in 2015 and turned them into a calendar. Then for each month we've prepared for you a little puzzle or story (or both) around something that we do or a service we've worked on.

If you solve a puzzle, let us know by email or twitter, and in December 2016 the highest scorers will get something for their Christmas stocking!

The calendar is available as a desk calendar, and if you don't have one, but really really want one, just drop us an email with your address, and we'll send one right out (while stocks last!).

Have yourselves a fantastic 2016!
