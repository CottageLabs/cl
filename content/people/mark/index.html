Mark is a founder and senior partner of Cottage Labs LLP, and a Doctor of Social Informatics with a PhD from the University of Edinburgh. He is in charge of CL sysadmin and numerous projects, particularly enjoying those with more exploratory aims. Mark is an expert in software architecture and development methodologies, coding in python and node/js, relational/nosql databases and indexing systems, agile project managemenet methodologies, and linux OS and related server software.

His academic interests range from philosophy to sociology via mathematics and distributed computing systems, and his current research explores an understanding of being as a technosocial phenomenon. The practical applications of this range from improved software development lifecycle to enhancing social decision making.

Mark is also an occasional tutor and mentor on software development courses, as well as a director of Content Mine Ltd. When not pursuing professional and academic goals, his pastimes mostly involve training in various forms of exerice - with current favourites being gymnastics and running.
